A question before we begin

If millions of Americans are already asking AI about their health every day — who should be answering?

The Reality
79% of US adults turn to the internet for health answers. Most find AI results reliable. The behaviour has changed. The standard hasn't.
The Challenge
Half of those same people don't trust AI to make decisions about their care. They want guidance — not a misdiagnosis. No one is offering them the difference.
The Potential
1,300+ FDA-cleared AI devices exist in the US — nearly all built for clinicians. A responsible, consumer-facing AI health guide built on real clinical measurement doesn't exist yet.
Our Answer
kai — Your Personal Health Guide AI. Built on 90 years of OMRON clinical precision. Not a doctor. A guide you can trust.
What kai is  ·  What kai is built on
What kai is

kai is named after 改 — the first character of kaizen. Continuous improvement through small, consistent steps.

For 90 years, this is how OMRON has built its hardware. kai brings the same discipline to AI — learning from every reading, improving over time.

A guide, not a doctor. kai never diagnoses or prescribes. It makes your measurements meaningful between appointments.

What kai is built on

kai runs on OMRON's 90-year clinical heritage and the Luscii platform — already trusted by hospitals across Europe.

No regulatory risk. kai is not a medical device. It works in the guidance layer — below the FDA clearance threshold, above the threshold that makes it useful.

The First Program — Why Pregnancy

Pregnancy is when women are most health-conscious — and most underserved. Blood pressure complications develop silently, between appointments. By the time a problem is found, the window for early action has often closed.

Women who develop preeclampsia carry more than double the long-term risk of heart disease and stroke — for decades after delivery.

kai monitors continuously. Clinically grounded. Every day.

Why the US, exclusively

The AI health category is forming here, fast — without clinical leadership. Consumer tools are growing under relaxed 2026 FDA guidelines, but without grounding.

How it reaches women

OMRON EVOLV — wireless, clinically validated for pregnancy — connects to kai at point of sale in designated pharmacies.

When a woman buys something pregnancy-related, kai is introduced as her personal health guide. Simple to set up. Immediate value from the first reading.
